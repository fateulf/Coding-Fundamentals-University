Exercise 3

Tropical cyclones are categorised on a scale of 1 to 5 according to their central pressure. A category 1 cyclone is the least severe and a category 5 cyclone is the most severe. Write a script that prompts the user to enter a cyclone category number and uses an alert box to display the corresponding effects as specified below:
1. Negligible house damage
2. Minor house damage
3. Some roof and structural damage
4. Significant roof loss and structural damage
5. Extremely dangerous with widespread destruction

Tropical Cyclone Category Effects
